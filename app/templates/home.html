From Seed to Your Kitchen

How It Works

Each microgreen tray follows a precise 7–9 day lifecycle, fully tracked in our system.

1

Seed

Premium seeds weighed to exactly 25g per tray. Supplier and lot tracked.

2

Blackout

3 days in darkness to develop strong root systems and vibrant colour.

3

Light

Moved to light for 4–6 days. We monitor daily for mold and growth.

4

Harvest

Cut, weighed, packed, and delivered fresh — typically within hours.
